'cgol2.py': Simple implementation of Conway's Game of Life.

Press 'p' to pause or unpause.
Press 's' to step through the evolution frame-by-frame.
Press 'm' to modify the canvas. Left-click to add cells and Right-click to kill cells.
Press 'a' to make the animation active again (i.e. not in any of the above modes).

Press any number from 1 to 9 to set the zoom/window size. (The default zoom level is 7x.)
